Owning a cat can be an extremely rewarding relationship.
A cat has the ability to both calm your nervous system and provide an immediate
outlet for fun and play. Although cats are independent animals who like to scavenge
and explore on their own terms, they are also very affectionate with their owners
and people they trust.

The versatility of cat ownership is one reason why so many people
enjoy feline companionship. Cats make great pets whether you live in
a big house or tiny apartment, and they provide all the fun and play of
larger animal companions.
